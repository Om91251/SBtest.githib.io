Search

It’s easy to find the products you need on SteelBuy. From the Search tab enter the product specifications for what you need, dimensions, grade and finish, then click submit.

Smart Match

After submitting your search, two lists are presented, one of exact matches, and the other is alternate suggestions. Our smart algorithm interprets your search criteria and offers other options you could use. Check the details, read the description and view the Test Certificates online.

Read more

Alerts

To never miss a buying opportunity in the future, you can create product alerts in one click. When you search for material and you think it is an item you will require again, simply click the Create Alert button in the top right corner on the Search page. You can create as many Alerts as you wish and will be notified as soon as a seller lists this material.

Read more

Buying

By pressing Buy Now, you can complete your payment process and confirm your order, after which you will receive an order confirmation email. On the checkout page, you can view your order summary, redirect the delivery to a different address, supply supplementary delivery instructions and enter a purchase order number (optional).

Sell more, sell smarter.

Listing product for sale on SteelBuy is easy and takes seconds.

Read more

1. Create a listing for sale

Listing your products is easy. Enter your details, collection address, product specification, expiry date for the listing and submit. Done!

2. Submit and approve

When you submit your listing you will receive an email approving or rejecting the listing. If it is rejected the reason will be provided and you can edit the details and resubmit.

3. Manage your listings

You can manage as many listings as you wish. We encourage all users to see SteelBuy as your free digital sales channel. Requiring little human input from you and your team, ideal for commodity sales.
